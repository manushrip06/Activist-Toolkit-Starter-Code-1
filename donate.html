Take Initiative

Donate!

The easiest way for you to take intiative is to donate to ocean pollution organizations like Team Seas, which was initiated by Mr. Beast. On the Team Seas website, for every 1 dollar donated, will remove 1 pound of trash from the ocean! This is the best way for you to help our world have cleaner oceans.

Fundraise!

Take the first step towards a change! Start a fundraiser to initiate the reduction in ocean pollution. Or maybe you can create your own organization. Let's preserve the beauty of our Earth's oceans!!
