Info About The Problem

What Causes Ocean Pollution?

Ocean Pollution is caused by human activites such as burning fossil fuels and polluting our oceans and rivers.

What Are The Effects Of Ocean Pollution?

Ocean pollution harms marine animals because of the burning of fossil fuels and littering trash on beaches and rivers which end up in the oceans, makes it hard for marine animals to thrive.

Why Should I care?

We should care because this problem effects us as well because ocean pollution harms marine animals and when we buy seafood it can harm us because the seafood we buy is unhealthy because of ocean pollution.This is why I believe it is important to care about this issue.
